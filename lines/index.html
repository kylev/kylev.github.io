Practice Line Generator

Work in Progress

This generates practices sheets for calligraphy. Or it will. It's not done yet.

I was inspired by Jeff Lee's pointed script guideline generator when I realize that it was not able to give me the 3:2:3 ratio lines I needed to work on Mastering Copperplate Calligraphy by Winters. Right now it is hard-coded to supply only the lines I need fo practice. I intend to expand it to have presets for popular methods, as well as a more flexible and prettier output.
